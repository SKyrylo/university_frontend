Understanding RAG

Retrieval-Augmented Generation: Enhancing AI with Your Knowledge

What is RAG?

RAG combines the power of large language models with your own data to provide more accurate and contextually relevant responses. It's like giving AI a personalized knowledge base to work with.

How It Works

When you ask a question, RAG first searches through your documents to find relevant information. Then, it uses this information to generate a response that's both accurate and specific to your context.

Benefits

  • More accurate responses based on your specific data
  • Reduced hallucinations and incorrect information
  • Better context understanding
  • Customized knowledge base

Components

  • Document Processing
  • Vector Database
  • Retrieval System
  • Language Model

The RAG Process

1

Document Upload

Upload your PDF documents to the system. The documents will be processed and indexed for efficient retrieval.

2

Question Input

Ask questions about your documents in natural language. The system will understand and process your query.

3

Context Retrieval

The system retrieves relevant information from your documents to provide context for the answer.

4

Answer Generation

Using the retrieved context, the system generates accurate and relevant answers to your questions.
